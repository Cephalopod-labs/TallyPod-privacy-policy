Introduction

Welcome to TallyPod ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information transparently and securely. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

By using TallyPod, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you use TallyPod, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and profile photo (when you sign in with Google)
  • Financial Records: Expense amounts, income sources, payment periods, categories, descriptions, and dates
  • Receipt Images: Photos of receipts you upload for OCR scanning
  • Preferences: Currency symbol, language, theme (dark/light mode), and view mode settings
  • Household Data: If you use the household sharing feature, we collect household names and member information

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version (for compatibility purposes only)
  • Usage Data: App features you use, to improve user experience (no tracking or analytics)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT use tracking cookies
  • We do NOT use analytics services (no Google Analytics, no Firebase Analytics)
  • We do NOT collect your location
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Store and manage your expenses, incomes, and receipts
  • OCR Processing: Extract text from receipt images using on-device Google ML Kit (images are processed locally on your device, not sent to external servers)
  • Cloud Sync: Sync your data across your devices using Supabase (secure cloud storage)
  • Authentication: Verify your identity using Google Sign-In
  • Household Sharing: If enabled, share expenses and incomes with household members you invite
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and support requests

We do NOT use your data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose not directly related to the app's functionality.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers (PostgreSQL database with encryption)
  • Local Storage: Some data is cached locally on your device for offline access (encrypted SQLite database)

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication: Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication via Google Sign-In
  • Access Control: Row-level security (RLS) ensures users can only access their own data
  • Biometric Protection: Optional fingerprint/Face ID lock for additional security

3.3 Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You can delete all your data at any time from the Profile screen ("Delete All Data" option)
  • If you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days

4. Third-Party Services

TallyPod uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Sign-In

  • Purpose: Authentication
  • Data Shared: Email, name, profile photo
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Supabase (Cloud Database)

  • Purpose: Data storage and synchronization
  • Data Shared: All user data (expenses, incomes, preferences)
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Location: Data is stored on servers in the United States

4.3 Google ML Kit (OCR)

  • Purpose: Text recognition from receipt images
  • Data Processing: All processing happens on your device (no data is sent to Google servers)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Household Members: If you use the household sharing feature, other members you invite can view shared expenses and incomes
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase and Google Sign-In as described above (they are bound by strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

6.1 General Rights (All Users)

  • Access: View all your data in the app
  • Correction: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Deletion: Delete all your data from Profile > Delete All Data
  • Export: Export your complete account data in machine-readable format (CSV/TXT) - FREE for all users (GDPR/CCPA compliance). Premium users also have access to advanced analytics reports with charts and trends.

6.2 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to be Forgotten: Request complete deletion of your data (contact us at cephalopodlabs@gmail.com)
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format (CSV)
  • Objection: Object to data processing (you can stop using the app and delete your data)
  • Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  • Lodge a Complaint: Contact your local data protection authority

6.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of sale of personal information (we do NOT sell your data)

6.4 Panama Law 81 of 2019

As a Panama-based app, we comply with Panama's Data Protection Law:

  • You have the right to access, rectify, cancel, and oppose (ARCO rights)
  • You can exercise these rights by contacting us at cephalopodlabs@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located in the United States (Supabase servers). By using TallyPod, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

11. Account Deletion

11.1 How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your TallyPod account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do this:

Method 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open TallyPod and go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  2. Scroll down to the Account Management section
  3. Tap "Delete All Data"
  4. Read the warning message carefully (this action is irreversible)
  5. Confirm deletion
  6. You will be signed out automatically

Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

  • Send an email to cephalopodlabs@gmail.com
  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your registered email address and reason (optional)
  • We will process your request within 48 hours

11.2 What Data is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Immediately from your device:
    • All expense records (receipts, amounts, categories, descriptions)
    • All income sources and payment periods
    • All receipt images stored locally
    • User preferences (theme, language, view mode)
    • Household memberships and invitations
    • Local SQLite database (completely wiped)
  • Within 30 days from cloud servers:
    • All data stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
    • User account information (email, name, profile photo)
    • All household data if you are the owner
  • Retained for legal compliance (90 days maximum):
    • Transaction logs (if applicable for premium subscriptions)
    • Payment records (required by tax and financial regulations)
    • These are anonymized and cannot be linked back to you
